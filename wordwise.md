# Privacy Policy for WordWise

**Last updated: April 2026**

WordWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application.

## Data We Collect
WordWise stores all user-generated data, such as practice progress, settings preferences, daily streaks, and XP/achievement state, locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal vocabulary progress on our external servers.

## Third-Party Services
We use Google AdMob as a third-party service to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect, use, and share certain information (such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, and usage data) to provide personalized and non-personalized ads. 

For more information on how Google uses the collected information, please visit the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Permissions
The app may request notification permissions to send you daily practice reminders. This feature is completely optional and can be managed or disabled at any time in the app's Settings or your device's system settings.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above for advertising purposes through Google AdMob.

## Children's Privacy
Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
